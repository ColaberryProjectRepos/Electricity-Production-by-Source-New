RenVsNonRenRatio KPI

1. From visualizations click Card visualization, drag

 ‘RenVsNonRenRatio’ card from 'MeasuresForFuel' to values fields.

2. In the format->visual -> callout value, set font Style = DIN, size=30, color= #ED7203 , display units =Auto, category label= off

3. In the format->general, set

Height=105, Width=228, Horizontal=0, Vertical=94,

Title=On

 Text=‘RenVsNonRenRatio’,  Front=DIN Size=16 , Text color =#FFFFFF, Background Color = #A386000 , alignment = Center

Effects-background=on, color= #A0D1FF, transparency=70%, visal border = on , color= #808080 , transparency=0%
