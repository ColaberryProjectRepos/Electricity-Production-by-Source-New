Therefore, my suggestions for reducing the production of non-renewable sources of energy and increasing the production of renewable energy.

Here are my suggestions on how to produce renewable energy:

  • Install solar panels on your rooftop: Solar panels are a great way to generate clean energy for your home or business. They convert sunlight into electricity that you can use to power your home or business.
  • Invest in wind turbines: Wind turbines generate energy from the wind, which is a renewable resource. If you live in an area with strong winds, investing in a wind turbine could be a good option.
  • Use hydropower: If you live near a river or other body of water, you could use hydropower to generate electricity. This involves using the energy of moving water to turn turbines and generate electricity.
  • Consider geothermal energy: Geothermal energy uses the heat from the Earth's core to generate electricity. This is a clean and renewable source of energy that is becoming more popular.


Here are some suggestions on how we should avoid producing non-renewable energy:

  • Invest in energy-efficient appliances: One of the best ways to reduce your reliance on non-renewable energy is to invest in energy-efficient appliances. These appliances use less energy and can help you save money on your energy bills.
  • Use public transportation: Transportation is one of the biggest sources of non-renewable energy consumption. Using public transportation or biking, walking, or carpooling are ways to reduce your reliance on non-renewable energy.
  • Support renewable energy policies: Supporting policies that promote renewable energy can help shift our energy use away from non-renewable sources. This can include policies like tax credits for renewable energy investments, incentives for energy-efficient buildings, and requirements for utilities to use more renewable energy.
  • Reduce energy waste: Reducing energy waste is an important step in reducing our reliance on non-renewable sources of energy. This can include things like turning off lights when you leave a room, using a programmable thermostat to control your heating and cooling, and using natural light instead of electric lights when is possible.
