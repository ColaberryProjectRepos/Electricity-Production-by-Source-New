Title and Logos 15

Colaberry Logo -

1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select the Colaberry logo and click open.

2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the bellow properties.

Height=115

Width=158

Horizontal= 12

Vertical =2

Title=off

Effects->Background=on, Transparency=0%



Electricity Logo -

1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select the Electricity logo and click open.

2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the bellow properties.

Height=94

Width=130

Horizontal =173

Vertical =0

Title=off

Effects->Background=on, Transparency=0%
