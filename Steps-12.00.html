Creating Measure Production and YOY 12

From the "Home" tab in ribbon, click "Enter Data" and just rename the blank table to "MeasuresForElectricity"  and click OK. Click on Measures table in the fields section and then right click and choose 'new measure' and enter 1 measure formula from below and click tick next to it to create that measure. Right click on the measure table to create another measure and repeat the same steps until all the below measures are created in "MeasuresForElectricity" table.

 

Measures:

1.    RenProd = SUM(Electricity[Renewable_Energy_Production])

2.    NonRenProd = SUM(Electricity[NonRenewable_Energy_Production])

3.    Solar Prod = SUM(Electricity[Electricity from solar (TWh)])

4.    Oil Prod = Sum(Electricity[Electricity from oil (TWh)])

5.    Wind Prod = Sum(Electricity[Electricity from wind (TWh)])

6.    Gas Prod = SUM(Electricity[Electricity from gas (TWh)])

7.    Other Renewable prod = Sum(Electricity[Electricity from other renewables (TWh)])

8.    Nuclear Prod = Sum(Electricity[Electricity from nuclear (TWh)])

9.    hydroProd = SUM(Electricity[Electricity from hydro (TWh)])

10. Coal prod = Sum(Electricity[Electricity from coal (TWh)])

11. Total Generate = SUM(Electricity[Total Generate])

12. RenVsNonRenRatio = DIVIDE( [Renewable YOY],[NonRenewable YOY])

 

13. Renewable YOY =

VAR Ren_CY=SUM(Electricity[Renewable_Energy_Production ])

VAR Ren_PrevY = CALCULATE(SUM(Electricity[Renewable_Energy_Production ]),SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR('Calendar'[Date]))

RETURN

IF(ISBLANK(Ren_PrevY),0,DIVIDE((Ren_CY-Ren_PrevY), Ren_PrevY))
